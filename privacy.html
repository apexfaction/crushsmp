Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Crush SMP ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. It applies to our Minecraft server (CrushSMPP.minehut.gg), our website, and our web store at crushsmp-webstore.tebex.io.

By using any of our services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you disagree with anything here, please stop using our services and contact us if you have concerns.

2. Information We Collect

We collect a limited amount of information to operate the server and process purchases. This includes:

  • Minecraft username and UUID — collected automatically when you join the server. Used to apply ranks, track punishments, and manage your account.
  • IP address — logged when you connect to the server. Used for security purposes, ban enforcement, and preventing alt account abuse.
  • Chat messages and commands — logged server-side for moderation purposes. Staff may review logs when investigating rule violations.
  • Purchase information — when you buy from our Tebex store, Tebex collects your payment details. We receive your Minecraft username and the item purchased. We do not receive or store your card details.
  • Discord user data — if you join our Discord, Discord's own privacy policy applies. We may store your Discord username if you submit a support ticket or application.
We do not collect your real name, email address, or physical address directly. Any personal data collected during checkout is handled by Tebex — see their privacy policy at tebex.io for details.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly to operate and improve Crush SMP. Specifically:

  • To apply purchased ranks and items to your account in-game.
  • To enforce server rules and manage bans and punishments.
  • To identify and prevent cheating, ban evasion, and abuse.
  • To respond to support requests and bug reports.
  • To monitor server performance and improve the player experience.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain server logs (including IP addresses and chat logs) for a reasonable period of time to allow for moderation and security investigations. This is typically up to 90 days, though ban-related records may be kept longer.

Purchase records are retained for as long as necessary to manage your rank and comply with legal obligations. If you would like your data removed, please contact us via Discord.

5. Third-Party Services

Crush SMP uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Minehut — our server is hosted on Minehut. Their infrastructure processes connection data. See minehut.com for their privacy policy.
  • Tebex — our store is powered by Tebex (Overwolf Ltd). All payment processing is handled by them. See tebex.io for their privacy policy.
  • Discord — our community hub. Governed by Discord's privacy policy at discord.com/privacy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their policies directly.

6. Cookies

Our main website does not use cookies or tracking scripts. Our Tebex store may use cookies to manage your shopping session and process payments. These are managed by Tebex, not by us.

7. Children's Privacy

Crush SMP is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately via Discord and we will take steps to delete that information.

Players under 18 should have parental permission before making any purchases through our store.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to know what data we hold about you.
  • The right to request deletion of your data (where technically and legally possible).
  • The right to correct inaccurate data.
  • The right to object to how we use your data.

To exercise any of these rights, open a ticket in our Discord at discord.gg/PAcpFsbv5p. We will respond as soon as reasonably possible.

Note that some data (such as ban records) may be retained even after a deletion request for legitimate security purposes.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we hold. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data and are not liable for any breach that occurs despite our reasonable precautions.

If you believe there has been a security issue involving your data, please contact us immediately via Discord.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We may also notify players of significant changes via our Discord server. Continued use of our services after any changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out through our Discord server at discord.gg/PAcpFsbv5p. Open a support ticket and a staff member will get back to you.
